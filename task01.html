Who We Are

A community-driven organization dedicated to compassionate change

Our Story

Founded on September 23, 2020, InAmigos Foundation started with a simple belief: every person deserves a chance to grow and thrive. What began as a small group of passionate volunteers has grown into a movement across India, touching thousands of lives.

What We Do

We work with communities to provide education, meals, job training, environmental care, and support for animals. We're not just here to give help—we're here to build partnerships and create lasting change.

Why We Do It

Because we believe that compassion and community can transform the world. We work transparently, listen to people, and measure our success by the real impact we create in people's lives.

Our Promise

What Drives Us

We serve communities with genuine compassion. Our mission is simple: create transformative change through education, care, empowerment, and environmental responsibility. We believe every person deserves dignity and every creature deserves compassion.

Where We're Headed

We dream of a world where education is for everyone, hunger doesn't exist, every person can provide for their family, and our planet is protected. We envision communities where people look out for each other and everyone has a real chance to thrive.
